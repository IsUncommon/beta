Progressive Web Apps

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) are Web applications that look and feel just like native applications. They can be installed to your users’ home screens, work offline, send push notifications, and have privileged access to certain APIs.

PWAs are a great alternative to native mobile apps, since they can be built once and run on every platform that has a modern Web browser. They give your users a near-native experience at less than half the cost of building native apps for all the major mobile platforms.

We can super-charge your web experiences by helping you design and build PWAs, or by helping you upgrade your existing Web apps to PWAs.

We’ve built Progressive Web Apps for our clients to satisfy a variety of business goals: whether it’s enabling new kinds of offline experiences, improving load times for existing applications, or increasing engagement by allowing users to install Web apps to their phone’s home screens.

An example of an offline experience that we built is StoryWeaver by Pratham Books.

Web Performance Optimization

If you are an online business, your conversion rate is directly linked to how fast your website loads and how fast users can navigate through it thereafter. Every additional second of delay in load time can cause 10% of your users to give up and close your page, negatively affecting your sales (source).

Our team has experience profiling and diagnosing performance issues on content-focused websites as well as JavaScript-heavy Web apps built with modern frameworks. We can help you interpret scores produced by WebPageTest and Lighthouse, and apply fixes to your code in order to bring them up to recommended standards. We’re also adept at interpreting data from the Google Chrome CPU and memory profilers, and using it to pinpoint problem areas in your app’s codebase.

We can even review your codebase and build pipelines in detail in order to discover insidious performance killers that are frustrating your users and causing conversion rates to drop.
