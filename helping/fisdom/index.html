Recent Work / Fisdom: Mobile Apps

Who says investing can't be fun!

We gave Fisdom’s Android and iOS apps a fun twist to make it appealing to a young userbase.

What we did

  • iOS app design
  • Android app design

Domain

Fisdom aims to help people invest intelligently without making it rocket science. To solve this, their investment process is reversed - you set a goal and start towards that amount in monthly steps.

If it’s not broken, don’t fix it!

During the discovery phase, we figured Fisdom had their major use-cases and flows nailed down quite well. What could be improved was a visual coherence in the app that would make it playful, fun to use.

Visual Oomph

Added visual associations with all goals and numbers. This way the user has a better way to plan for his investments.

Friendly error messages, Smart Empty States & Feedbacks

Friendly and fun error messages, and smart empty states that guide users towards actions.
